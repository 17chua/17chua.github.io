Post: 10/6/23

I can't believe that week 7 of the semester has already gone by! For fall 2023, I’m taking systems programming, yoga, intro to AI, and a class on text-based games. I found it amusing when I learned about and played the text-based game Zork in my game design class, and the next week it came up in the natural language lecture in my AI class. I’ve found this semester to be a lot more hands-on and program-heavy than last semester.

Besides school and studying, I’ve picked up drawing again this semester. I used to draw a lot in high school, and I found my old portfolio in the basement when I went home for Labor Day weekend. I joined the Life Drawing Club at UIUC and drew more in my free time. This semester was my first time drawing live models, except for one time in my art class in high school when we drew our classmates for an activity. I’m still using the same newsprint pad from when I was 15. Here’s my progress!
